About Sanbat Platinum Hotel

Sanbat Platinum Hotel the Most Recommended Hotel All Over Saudi Arabia

Explore Jazan through our hotel (Sanbat Platinum) Welcome to Sanbat Platinum Hotel in Jizan, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. The hotel enjoys a strategic location, as it is located in the central area, next to the Sky Mall complex and the street of restaurants. It is 3 km from the airport and 2.50 km from Jizan port. The hotel includes sixty-two modern hotel units, including standard rooms, bridal suites, family suites, and rooms for people of determination. The hotel contains several services (restaurant, coffee shop, laundry) and 24-hour housekeeping, thus providing convenience for guests, especially business guests. The hotel has a health club and a shopping center, giving comfort and effort to families. The complimentary breakfast for hotel guests can be enjoyed in the restaurant (Teen and Olive), where the comfort is. The hotel provides free internet in the rooms.
